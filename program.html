Emergency Relief

A principle operation of The Gathering is the emergency relief program.

The emergency relief program is operated in conjunction with the Baptist Bush Church.

Emergency relief program outlets are operated at each of the location listed below.From these outlets people may access food parcels and other emergency relief support including, variety of social services, mentoring, job-ready training, preparation of resumes, basic budgeting counselling and interagency referrals.From these outlets children’s craft groups and social activities for adults also operate.

Activities include darts, pool competitions, or just a chat. Clients accessing these outlets are encouraged to participate in other activities of the Gathering’s sub-groups. The Gathering is also registered a facilitator for those carrying out community work orders.

ACE Trades Training

Alternative Christian Education, Trades Training and Mentoring Program

The Alternative Christian Education (ACE) Trades Training and Mentoring Program introduces participants to the construction industry. The ACE Program is designed to accommodate people with low literacy levels and from various cultural backgrounds. The ACE Program is very versatile and can be adapted to suit individual circumstances.

Nationally Accredited Training

Certificate I in Construction (CPC10111)

Certificate II in Construction (CPC20112)

General Construction Induction ‘White Card’ for working in the construction industry

Graduates of the course will meet the accreditation requirements for either Certificate I or II in Construction, as asssessed by, and in collaboration with the Australian College of Career Development (Edubiz International Ltd- RTO 31980)

The Program Aims

Build up participants spiritually and physically in Jesus’name by training them for employment in the construction industry.

Provide training for participants to obtain a Certificate I and II in Construction

Provide on the job training, work experience, work references, employment opportunities or continued trades training.

Assist participants in fulfilling basic requirements to become employed e.g. tax file number.

Assist participants in obtaining a driver’s license and accommodation with the absence of drugs, alcohol and gambling.

Address anti-social behaviour and crime prevention through participants having respect for other people’s property and pride in their painting work as they start to improve community buildings.

Youth Services

The Gathering Incorporation operates a variety of youth services including:

Homework Help, Mentoring and Support Service, Youth Accommodation

Youth Station - Supported Youth Accommodation

Nature of the accommodation:

The youth accommodation is a safe, secure, supported environment.

The accommodation provided is medium to long term, 3-6 months

Short term emergency accommodation may also be provided, 3-6 days

Support Offered:

Support that the Youth Accommodation offers includes the following

Budget assistance and Networking with other agencies

Emergency service food parcels and Cooking and nutrition classes

Access to health education information, Counselling and referrals

Tutoring and computer literacy training and Aid in becoming job-ready

Child, youth and family intervention services

Recreation and sports activities including fitness classes

Aid accessing bond assistance and alternative accommodation
